About

Future Holotape is a Los Angeles based cyberpunk/synthwave band. The duo brings a new style that combines the future and the past, creating a sound that will make you feel like you're driving down the streets of 2040.

Future Holotape released its first album Offset in 2012. They have since released three more studio albums: Analog Renegades, Terra District 1, and Dreaming of Connections

Future Holotape has toured across the Southwest United States and continues to experience massive success and popularity
